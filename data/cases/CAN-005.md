# CAN-005: Gander NL 1951 — U.S. Navy Pilot Close Encounter

**Date:** 1951
**Location:** Gander, Newfoundland, Canada (48.9°N, 54.5°W)
**Classification:** RV — Radar-Visual Correlation | Military Encounter

## Case Summary

In 1951, a U.S. Navy pilot operating in the Gander, Newfoundland area reported a close encounter with a bright orange circular object during flight operations. The encounter was documented in U.S. Navy Records and represents one of the earliest military pilot UFO sightings in Canada.

Gander was strategically important during the early Cold War period. RCAF Station Gander served as a critical refueling and monitoring point for transatlantic flights, and the area was under constant military surveillance. This makes the sighting particularly significant.

## Historical Context — Gander in 1951

In 1951, Newfoundland had only recently joined Confederation with Canada (March 31, 1949). Gander was home to a major RCAF station and was a crucial node in North American air defense:

- The DEW Line (Distant Early Warning) was being planned, and Gander was part of the early radar network
- U.S. military aircraft regularly operated from Gander as part of NORAD predecessor organizations
- The area was under active military surveillance due to Cold War tensions

## The Encounter

A U.S. Navy pilot on a flight in the Gander area observed a bright orange circular/luminous object. Key details:

- **Shape:** Circular or disc-shaped
- **Color:** Bright orange — described as luminous or glowing
- **Proximity:** Close enough to the aircraft for detailed observation
- **Behavior:** The object appeared to be operating in the same airspace as the military aircraft

The pilot documented the encounter, and the report was filed through U.S. Navy channels. The case was later referenced in Project Magnet files (the Canadian government's official UFO study program).

## Project Magnet Connection

Project Magnet was the Canadian government's official UFO research program, run by Wilbert Smith of the Department of Transport from 1950-1954. Smith was a senior radio engineer who took UFO reports seriously and investigated military sightings with particular interest.

The Gander 1951 case was referenced in Project Magnet files, indicating that Smith considered it worthy of attention in his investigation of aerial phenomena.
