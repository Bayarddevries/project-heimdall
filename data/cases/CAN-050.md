# CAN-050: 2025 Sheridan Lake, BC — Pulsating Orb Interference

## Case Summary

In 2025, campers near Sheridan Lake, British Columbia observed a pulsating orb in the night sky that caused interference with cell phones and other electronic devices. The event was documented by the Canadian UFO Survey and represents one of the most recent UFO encounters in the archive, notable for the electromagnetic effects on personal electronics.

**Observation Details:**
- **Object:** Pulsating orb — a spherical or near-spherical light source with a pulsating/pulsing behavior
- **Electromagnetic interference:** Campers experienced interference with cell phones during the sighting
- **Location:** Sheridan Lake area in the BC interior (Chilcotin region)
- **Witnesses:** Multiple campers
