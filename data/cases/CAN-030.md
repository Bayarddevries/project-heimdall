# CAN-030: L'Ancienne-Lorette
**Date:** 1987
**Location:**  (46.8, -71.4)
**Classification:** CE1

## Case Summary

In 1987, two Quebec provincial police (Sureté du Quebec) officers on highway patrol near L'Ancienne-Lorette, Quebec witnessed a hovering disc-shaped object with rotating multicolored lights. The object emitted a low-frequency hum before accelerating silently upward and disappearing into the cloud layer. Both officers signed a joint affidavit and filed an official SQ incident report. Law enforcement witnesses give this case high credibility.
