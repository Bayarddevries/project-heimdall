# CAN-047: 2024 Hafford, SK — Domed Disc Over Farmland

**Date:** 2024
**Location:** Hafford, Saskatchewan, Canada (52.8°N, 107.3°W)
**Classification:** CE1 — Close Encounter | Classic Dome Disc

## Case Summary

In 2024, a witness near Hafford, Saskatchewan reported a close-range observation of a domed disc — the classic "flying saucer" shape — hovering at low altitude over farmland. The case was documented by MUFON (Mutual UFO Network) as Case #13582, including a formal field investigator report.

## The Sighting

**Observation Details:**
- **Shape:** Classic domed disc — a flat circular body with a dome or protrusion on top
- **Position:** Low-altitude hover over an agricultural field
- **Movement:** Stationary hover; the object remained in place during observation
- **Visibility:** Close enough for the witness to clearly see the domed disc shape and surface details
- **Duration:** Extended enough to observe details and report to MUFON
