# CAN-055: Vulcan County (Pilot Encounter)
**Date:** Feb 2025
**Location:**  (50.5, -113.2)
**Classification:** RV

## Case Summary

In February 2025, a commercial pilot on approach over Vulcan County, Alberta reported a fast-moving, non-transponder object pacing the aircraft at a parallel altitude for approximately 12 miles. The object then executed an impossible vertical climb and vanished from ATC radar. Air Traffic Control confirmed there were no scheduled military exercises in the area at the time. The incident was filed with Transport Canada's Aviation Safety Reporting System.
