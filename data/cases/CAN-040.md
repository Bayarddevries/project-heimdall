# CAN-040: 2008 Niagara Falls, ON — Sheraton Hotel MIB Encounter

**Date:** 2008
**Location:** Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada (43.0°N, 79.0°W)
**Classification:** CE1 — Close Encounter | MIB (Men in Black) Follow-up

## Case Summary

In 2008, security personnel at the Sheraton Hotel in Niagara Falls, Ontario observed luminous orbs in the night sky over the famous tourist destination. Following the sighting, "Men in Black" (MIB) — unidentified men in dark suits — reportedly appeared at the hotel in connection with the incident.

## The Orb Observation

**Security Personnel Observation:**
- **Object:** Luminous orbs observed in the sky
- **Location:** Visible from the Sheraton Hotel grounds
- **Context:** Niagara Falls — one of the most well-lit tourist areas in Canada
- **Witnesses:** Hotel security staff (trained observers; responsible for monitoring)

## CCTV Footage

The Sheraton Hotel maintained CCTV systems:
- CCTV may have captured the orbs in the night sky
- Security personnel reviewed footage following the sighting
- The footage was documented as part of the hotel's security report

## The MIB Element

What makes this case unusual is the MIB follow-up:
- "Men in Black" are a well-documented but poorly understood UFO sub-phenomenon
- MIBs typically appear after a UFO sighting, often attempting to gather information or discourage witnesses
- In this case, unidentified men in dark suits reportedly appeared at the Sheraton Hotel following the orb sighting
- Their identity, affiliation, and purpose were never confirmed

## Assessment

**Strengths:**
- Security personnel witnesses (trained observers)
- Possible CCTV evidence
- MIB follow-up (documented in MIB case files)
- High-traffic tourist location (multiple potential corroborating witnesses)

**Weaknesses:**
- MIB cases are inherently difficult to verify
- No public release of CCTV footage
- No official government response confirmed
