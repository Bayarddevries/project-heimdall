# CAN-016: 1969 Prince George, BC — Yellow Sphere Sighting

**Date:** 1969
**Location:** Prince George, British Columbia, Canada (53.9°N, 122.7°W)
**Classification:** CE1 — Close Encounter | Visual Observation

## Case Summary

Three witnesses in Prince George, British Columbia observed a yellow spherical object hovering at low altitude in 1969. The event was reported to the RCMP, who filed a formal report. Prince George is the largest city in northern BC, and the sighting was made in a populated area.

## The Event

**Observation Details:**
- **Object:** Yellow spherical object
- **Behavior:** Hovering or moving very slowly at low altitude
- **Visibility:** Clear enough for three people to observe and identify as spherical
- **Location:** Prince George area — northern BC's largest urban center
- **Reporting:** The witnesses contacted the RCMP, who filed a report

## RCMP Reporting Context

The fact that this sighting generated an RCMP report is significant:
- RCMP officers in the 1960s took UFO reports seriously
- A report being filed means the witnesses were credible and specific
- Prince George had an established RCMP detachment
- The northern region of BC produced a notable number of UFO reports

## Assessment

**Strengths:**
- Three independent witnesses
- RCMP report filed
- Clear shape and color identification (yellow sphere)

**Weaknesses:**
- No physical evidence
- No radar confirmation
- Limited follow-up investigation documented
