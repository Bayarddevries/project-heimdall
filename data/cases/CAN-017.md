# CAN-017: January 1, 1970 Duncan, BC — Nurse Kendall's Close Encounter

**Date:** January 1, 1970 (New Year's Day)
**Location:** Duncan, British Columbia (Vancouver Island) (48.7°N, 123.7°W)
**Classification:** CE3 — Close Encounter of the Third Kind (Entity Contact)

## Case Summary

On New Year's Day 1970, Nurse Doreen Kendall of Duncan, British Columbia experienced a close encounter with a domed saucer craft and observed two tall men in dark suits through a window of the craft. The case is one of the most detailed CE3 encounters in Canadian history and was documented by authorities who conducted a psychological evaluation of the witness.

## The Witness

Doreen Kendall was a nurse — a trained medical professional. This is significant:
- She had medical training and observational skills
- She was not a UFO enthusiast or predisposed to such reports
- A psychological evaluation was conducted and she was assessed as credible

## The Encounter

**Sequence of Events (New Year's Day, 1970):**

1. Kendall observed an unusual domed saucer craft near her residence in Duncan
2. The craft was close enough for detailed observation
3. Through a window or opening in the craft, she saw **two tall men in dark suits**
4. The beings were humanoid ("tall men" in "dark suits")
5. The encounter was sufficiently detailed and disturbing that authorities conducted a psychological evaluation

## Entity Description

- **Number:** 2 beings
- **Height:** Tall
- **Appearance:** Humanoid, wearing dark suits
- **Context:** Seen through a craft window
- **Behavior:** Not described as threatening; simply observed

## Psychological Evaluation

The psychological profile conducted on Kendall was a standard procedure for extraordinary claims:
- The evaluation confirmed the witness was mentally stable and credible
- The detailed description was consistent and coherent
- The psychological assessment supported the witness's reliability

## Assessment

**Strengths:**
- Credible professional witness (nurse)
- Detailed entity description
- Psychological evaluation confirmed credibility
- CE3 encounters are among the rarest and most significant UFO categories
- Specific date (New Year's Day 1970) aids in documentation

**Weaknesses:**
- Single primary witness
- No physical evidence
- Entity encounters are inherently subjective

## Agent Notes
The Duncan, BC encounter shares similarities with other Canadian CE3 cases: the Duncan encounter with the tall beings parallels the entity descriptions in other cases. The "tall man" motif appears in several Canadian encounters. The psychological evaluation is a distinguishing feature that adds credibility.
