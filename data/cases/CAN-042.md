# CAN-042: 2012 Hamilton, ON — V-Formation over Ontario

**Date:** 2012
**Location:** Hamilton, Ontario, Canada (43.2°N, 79.8°W)
**Classification:** NL — Nocturnal Lights | V-Formation Phenomenon

## Case Summary

In 2012, dozens of witnesses across the Greater Hamilton area observed a V-shaped formation of bright lights moving slowly across the Ontario sky. The formation was recorded in multiple high-quality videos that were uploaded to YouTube, providing some of the best visual documentation of a V-formation sighting in Canadian history.

## The V-Formation

**Characteristics:**
- **Pattern:** Classic V-shaped formation of lights
- **Spacing:** Objects maintained precise, fixed spacing throughout the flight
- **Movement:** Slow, steady transit across the sky
- **Sound:** No sound reported (silent operation)
- **Color:** Bright white lights

## Video Documentation

Multiple witnesses captured the formation on video:
- **YouTube uploads:** At least one high-quality video was uploaded to YouTube
- **Multi-angle:** The formation was visible enough to be captured from multiple locations
- **Quality:** The videos show clearly defined lights in a V pattern

## The V-Formation Phenomenon

V-formations are among the most commonly reported multi-object UFO configurations worldwide:
- They appear in reports from every continent
- The precise, coordinated movement of multiple objects maintaining fixed spacing is difficult to explain with conventional aircraft (which would require multiple aircraft flying in formation — unusual for civilian aircraft and unlikely to be silent)
- Military formations are possible but would be unusual without authorization and would generate NOTAM alerts

## Conventional Explanations Considered
- **Military aircraft formation:** Possible, but unusual without NOTAM or authorization; would not be silent
- **Advertising drones:** Not available in 2012
- **LED-equipped consumer drones:** Not capable of the scale, distance, and coordination visible across the Hamilton region in 2012
- **Stars/planets:** V formations of lights don't match celestial patterns

## Assessment

**Strengths:**
- Dozens of witnesses
- Video documentation (YouTube)
- Multi-angle capture possibility
- Clear V-formation pattern

**Weaknesses:**
- Military formation possibility cannot be fully ruled out
- No radar data
